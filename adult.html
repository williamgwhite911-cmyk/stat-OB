⚠ Adult difficult airway considerations
Anticipate: obesity (ramped position), beard/dentition, c-collar in trauma, full stomach (RSI), age-related fragility.
Mitigations: ramped position, aggressive pre-O2 (NRB + apneic NC at 15 L/min), VL preferred, BIAD ready, surgical airway plan briefed.

PHI Formulary Note
✓ Ketamine — preferred RSI induction (1.5-2 mg/kg IV; max RSI 2 mg/kg). Max infusion 4 mg/kg/hr adult.
✓ Rocuronium — PHI standing order, 1.5 mg/kg IBW IV or 2 mg/kg IM.
△ Succinylcholine — PHI standing order is for Dangerous Agitation (4 mg/kg IM). Avoid in OB or with mag.
✗ Etomidate — NOT on PHI formulary. Reference only.
